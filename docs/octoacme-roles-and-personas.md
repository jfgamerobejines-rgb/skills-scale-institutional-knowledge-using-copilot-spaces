# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
Designs user interfaces and experiences, ensuring usability and accessibility. Collaborates with Product Managers and Developers to translate requirements into intuitive workflows.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and final UI assets
- Validate designs through user feedback and usability tests
- Maintain design system consistency

### Goals
- Deliver user-friendly, visually consistent product experiences

### Typical Communication
- Shares mockups during sprint planning
- Reviews feature implementation with Developers and Product Management

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Transforms project and user data into actionable insights that inform planning and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain project-related metrics
- Analyze trends and recommend improvements
- Advise Product and Project Managers on data-driven decisions

### Goals
- Enable data-informed planning and product iteration

### Typical Communication
- Provides analysis reports to project teams and stakeholders

---

## Tech Lead

### Role Summary
Provides technical direction, oversees architecture, and ensures code quality across the team.

### Responsibilities
- Guide system design and technical decisions
- Mentor developers and review code
- Coordinate technical approach with DevOps and QA

### Goals
- Ensure technical excellence and maintainability

### Typical Communication
- Facilitates technical discussions and decision logs

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Automates, monitors, and supports software delivery and production environments.

### Responsibilities
- Implement CI/CD pipelines
- Maintain deployment automation and infrastructure
- Monitor and respond to production issues

### Goals
- Ensure rapid, stable, and repeatable releases

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Developers and QA on build/test/deploy workflows

---

## QA Automation Engineer

### Role Summary
Develops and maintains automated tests to ensure product quality and reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain automated test suites
- Collaborate on test plans and acceptance criteria
- Report and track bugs found in CI/CD pipelines

### Goals
- Drive continuous improvement in test coverage and quality

### Typical Communication
- Works with Devs/PMs to refine acceptance criteria and test scenarios

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Acts as a bridge between end-users and the project team, bringing customer feedback into the improvement loop.

### Responsibilities
- Aggregate user feedback and incident reports
- Provide input on prioritization based on support trends
- Help document known issues and workarounds

### Goals
- Improve customer satisfaction and product reliability

### Typical Communication
- Regular feedback sessions with Product/Project Managers
- Channels urgent issues to appropriate team members
